S5 Sportsback Specifications

Powerful and attractive.
The Audi S5 Sportback.

The S5 Sportback offers plenty of space and the power of a sports car.
An enlarged interior and a high-power engine make this five-door vehicle into something
very special. What makes the S5 Sportback such an impressive sight are its design language and S-specific exterior elements.



What makes the big picture? Every detail

Standard S-specific elements on the exterior of the Audi S5 Sportback underscore it's sporty design: S-bumpers, radiator grille with honeycomb design in matt titanium black with inserts in matt aluminium silver, wide air inlets in the frame and blade in matt aluminium silver at the front. At the sides, there are the impressive 48.26 centimeter alloy wheels and the S-sport suspension as well as exterior mirror housings in aluminium look and the illuminated door sill trims with S-emblem at the front. And emphasising the width of the rear are the horizontal trim in matt aluminium silver surrounding the S diffuser, the S-specific twin tailpipes in chrome and the S-rear spoiler.
